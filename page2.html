The Beginnings

In 2020, the world was in shambles. School was closed, you couldn't go outside, and the world was on lockdown. I would go to virtual class, then play video games the rest of the day. I was also eating like a cow! Every day my mother would cook delicious foods that were impossible to resist. I was eating so much that I probably gained 20 pounds in 2 months! Life was kind of fun.
